About Us

Who are we?



Bruce Betts - Founder and CEO, Photographer

Bruce has been interested in photography for several years and has really started shooting in 2013. He drives La Photographie’s vision, to extend the precious experiences of people through photographs and to celebrate and share these beautiful life’s memories to other people.


Madylene Guevara - Photographer

Mady is literally addicted to making photos. She can turn ugly places into beautiful portraits through her exceptional skills in photography and editing.


Clarisse Centeno - Assistant Photographer

Clarisse really loves being creative, not only in taking pictures, She loves working with both still and moving images. She tries to involve the viewer in the images she takes.


Hannah Fong - Photo Editor

- Hannah sees photo editing as just adding some more cream on a cake. Editing is not cheating; it is a way to enhance the already existing elements of an image. For her, retouching photos help present the team’s projects in the best light possible.
