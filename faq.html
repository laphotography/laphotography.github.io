Frequently Asked Questions

What do people typically ask?

  1. How did you learn photography?

    2. Our passion for photography probably started way back in high school partly because of the influence of our artistically inclined relatives and mostly out of pure desire. Almost all of us were self taught

  2. What's your photography style?

    3. Bruce specializes on portraits, Mady is really great at Street photography and may even be better than Bruce at Portraits.

  3. What type of camera/equipment do you use?

    4. Mady guevara uses sony a5100 , Bruce uses the Nikon D90.

  4. Where are the best places to shoot?

    5. As a photographer, any place would be suitable to shoot in as long as you have vision and creativity. We can make it work no matter how mundane or extravagant a place seems. It can also depend on the client’s ideal location or theme.

  5. For you, what is the most significant element when taking pictures?

    6. When it comes to photography, the most essential element is that “captivating moment” or the moment you decide to take a picture.

  6. Do you edit/touch up all the images you’ve taken?

    7. Most of the time, yes we do. Post processing as you can observe gives more life to the original photos. The applications that we use for post processing are adobe photoshop/ lightroom.

  7. What should we wear to shoot?

    8. Whatever makes you comfortable,unless the shoot is based on a desired theme e.g 90s vibe. There are no restrictions when it comes to shoots. clients/models should just express their true selves because we, photographers are just responsible to capture the moment.

  8. What are your prices and packages?

  9. How do we reserve our portrait dates?

    10. You can set an appointment through social media, or text any one of us.

  10. Does your studio provide videography services?

    11. Yes we do. in fact, some of us actually attended workshops and classes related to videography and video editing.

  11. I’m on a budget, can you give me a discount?

    12. Let's see what we can work with, contact us.
